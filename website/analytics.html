Real User Analytics Dashboard

Insights from subscription trends, engagement behavior, and revenue performance.

Executive Summary

This analytics report provides insights into PulseFit user demographics, subscription behavior, engagement trends, and revenue generation. The Power BI dashboard highlights how users interact with the platform and which plan tiers contribute the most to revenue growth.

Problem Statement

PulseFit is experiencing rapid user growth but lacks visibility into who its users are, how they interact with the platform, which subscription plans they prefer, and how much revenue is being generated. This analysis provides clear insights to guide product improvement, marketing strategy, and revenue optimization.

Business Objective

The primary goal is to understand user growth, monitor subscription performance, identify high-value markets, and analyze the profitability of each plan tier. This enables the business to optimize marketing, enhance product experience, and strengthen overall revenue strategy.

Key Metrics Overview

  • Total Users
  • Revenue Generated
  • Conversion Rate
  • Subscription Distribution
  • Engagement Activity

User Demographics

  • Nigeria and South Africa account for the largest share of total users.
  • Egypt, Ghana, and Tanzania make up the remaining user distribution.
  • PulseFit is heavily adopted in West and Southern Africa.
  • This provides a strong base for targeted regional marketing.

Engagement Behaviour

  • Active sessions are consistent across most days.
  • Peak activity appears around midday and early evening.
  • User drop-offs happen mostly late at night when engagement is lowest.
  • Timing insights can guide better notification and workout reminder scheduling.

Conversion & Subscription Analysis

  • Basic plan attracts the most sign-ups but has lower retention.
  • Pro plan balances affordability and value, driving stable conversions.
  • Elite plan users show the highest loyalty and longest retention.
  • Premium-focused incentives may increase long-term subscriber value.

Revenue & Profitability

  • Elite plan users generate the highest share of total revenue.
  • Although fewer in number, Elite contributes roughly 40–50% of all earnings.
  • Pro plan is the second-largest revenue driver.
  • Strengthening premium offerings can significantly increase overall profitability.

Recommendations

  • Invest more marketing in high-performing regions.
  • Boost engagement through reminders and challenges.
  • Introduce discount campaigns to increase upgrades.
  • Track cohorts for deeper retention insight.

Future Enhancements

  • Automated Power BI refresh using gateways
  • AI forecasting for subscription trends
  • Retention and churn analysis dashboards
  • Profit forecasting using historical behavior
